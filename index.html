The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) is the principal national governing body of cricket in India.[13] Its headquarters is situated at the Cricket Centre in Churchgate, Mumbai.[14] The BCCI is the wealthiest governing body of cricket in the world.[15][16][17] The BCCI is involved in talent development through grassroots programs and cricket academies. Its initiatives include infrastructure development, coaching, and player welfare programs designed to maintain and enhance India's competitive performance internationally
